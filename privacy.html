Privacy Policy

This policy is effective as of 11th November 2022

1. Introduction

At Lolly Tots Day Nursery, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data of our customers, parents, and visitors. Our goal is to provide a safe environment for your child while ensuring transparency and responsibility in how we manage personal information.

Whether you're contacting us for inquiries or enrolling your child, we handle all data with the utmost care and respect for your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information you provide to us

We collect personal information directly from you when you interact with us, whether through inquiries, registration, or any other forms of communication. The information you provide helps us deliver better services and ensure a safe and nurturing environment for your child.

The types of information you may provide include:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and other necessary details that allow us to respond to inquiries or process applications.

  • Child's Information: The name, age, and other relevant details of your child, essential for enrollment and tailored nursery services.

  • Payment Details:: Any payment information related to fee inquiries or transactions, which we handle with strict confidentiality.

  • Communication Preferences: Any preferences or requests for updates on your child’s progress, open days, and other nursery events.

This information helps us manage our relationship with you, ensuring that you receive the best possible service and support from Lolly Tots Day Nursery.

2.2. Basic Website Interaction Data

While we do not actively track detailed information about your visit, some basic data may be automatically collected when you access our website. This data is used to ensure that our website functions properly and provides a good experience for all users.

Types of data collected may include:

  • General Website Traffic: We may gather basic information such as the number of visitors to our website and general usage patterns. This helps us understand overall interest in our services.

  • Browser Compatibility: To ensure the website is displayed correctly, we may collect anonymous data about the browser or device you're using.

This information is not linked to individual users and is used solely to maintain and improve the performance of our website.

2.3. User Comments and Submitted Content

We value feedback and input from our visitors. If you choose to leave comments or submit inquiries via our contact forms, we collect and store the information you provide in order to respond effectively and improve our services.

Types of content we may collect include:

  • Comments and Messages: Any feedback or questions submitted through our website forms or email communication are securely stored and used solely to address your needs.

  • Media Submissions: If you send us media files, such as photos or documents, in relation to your child’s enrollment or inquiries, these files will only be used to support your request and will be handled with confidentiality.

We do not share or publish any personal content without explicit consent.

2.4. Information from Other Sources

Occasionally, we may receive information from external sources to enhance the services we provide. This may include public information or details provided by trusted partners to assist in enrollment, support, or service improvement.

For instance, if you’ve been referred to us by another parent or third-party service, we may collect basic information about your referral to help streamline your experience. All third-party data is treated with the same level of care as the information you provide directly to us.

3. Types of Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to ensure we provide the best possible care and services for your child. The information collected helps us manage enrollment, communicate effectively, and deliver personalized services tailored to your family’s needs.

The types of information we collect include:

1.Personal Information: This includes basic details such as your name, contact information, and your child’s age, which helps us arrange visits, communicate updates, and manage admissions.

2.Preferences and Interests: We may ask about your child’s preferences, interests, or specific needs to tailor activities and care plans to your child's unique requirements.

3.Health and Dietary Information: For the safety and well-being of your child, we may collect information about allergies, medical conditions, or dietary restrictions to ensure we provide appropriate care.

4. How We Use Personal Information

We use the personal information you provide to help us deliver high-quality care and services for your child. The data enables us to ensure safety, keep you informed, and create a nurturing environment that caters to your child’s unique needs.

Specifically, we use this information for:

1.Communication: To contact you regarding admissions, scheduling visits, or updating you on your child's progress and nursery events.

2.Tailored Care: We use information such as health and dietary preferences to ensure your child’s well-being by providing the appropriate care and meals.

3.Safety and Compliance: Your personal details help us maintain a safe environment and comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4.1. Through Emails and Contact Forms

We collect and use information shared through emails and contact forms to respond to your inquiries, provide details about our services, and arrange tours or consultations as requested. This helps us stay connected with parents and ensure we meet your needs effectively.

5. Pricing and Taxes

  • All prices provided for our services are clear and inclusive of any applicable taxes unless otherwise stated. We ensure transparency in our pricing to help parents make informed decisions.

  • Should there be any changes to pricing or taxes, we will notify parents in advance to avoid any surprises in fees or payments.

We strive to maintain fair and competitive pricing for all our services. If you have any questions regarding the cost of our nursery services, please feel free to contact us for clarification.

5.1 Children's Privacy

We take the privacy of children very seriously. Any personal information collected about the children in our care, such as names, birth dates, or medical information, is handled with the highest level of confidentiality and used only for purposes directly related to their care and education.

We do not share children's personal information with third parties unless required by law or with explicit consent from the child's parent or legal guardian. Our goal is to ensure a safe, secure, and private environment for all children at our nursery.

6. Data Shared with Third Parties

At Lolly Tots Day Nursery, we respect your privacy and only share personal information with third parties under specific circumstances. This may include service providers who help us manage operations such as payment processing or IT support, all of whom are obligated to protect your data and use it only for the agreed-upon services.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Any data shared is done in compliance with applicable privacy laws, and we ensure that necessary safeguards are in place to protect the security and confidentiality of your information.

Should there be any legal obligations to share your information, such as in cases of law enforcement requests, we will only do so when required by law, ensuring that your rights and privacy are respected.

7. Security Measures

At Lolly Tots Day Nursery, we prioritize the security of your personal information. We implement a variety of technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure servers, and regular security assessments to ensure our systems remain robust against potential threats.

We also train our staff on data protection best practices and establish strict access controls to limit data access to only those who need it for their roles. While we strive to protect your information, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

8. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through our website or via other means, such as email, to ensure you are aware of the updates.

We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy for the latest information on our privacy practices. Your continued use of our services following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of such changes.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please do not hesitate to contact us.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us at nfo@lollytotsdaynursery.com.
