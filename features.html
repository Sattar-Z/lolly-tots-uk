Freshly Prepared Meals Daily

At Lolly Tots, we freshly prepare nutritious meals every day, ensuring that each child enjoys a balanced, healthy menu from breakfast through to afternoon snacks and tea. Our meals are designed to support growth and well-being, offering children a variety of fresh, wholesome foods.

Diverse and Exciting Meal Plans

Our meal plans introduce children to cuisines from around the world, with themed days like Meatless Mondays, International Wednesdays, and Fish Fridays. These thoughtfully curated menus encourage children to explore diverse flavors in a fun and mindful way.

We are committed to fostering a love for healthy eating, ensuring our little ones are well-fed and excited to try new dishes every day.

Enriching Experiences

After-School Clubs & Extracurricular Activities

Lolly Tots is excited to introduce a variety of extracurricular classes, providing dynamic opportunities for children to explore new skills and interests. These programs are designed to take a holistic approach to nurturing children’s intellectual, physical, emotional, and social development, while ensuring that learning remains fun and engaging.

Contact Us
Creative Dance Classes

Our Creative Dance classes encourage expression through movement, helping children develop physical coordination and rhythm. These activities foster spatial awareness, boost confidence, and enhance body control—essential skills during early development.

Self-Care Days

Our Self-Care Days teach children mindfulness, relaxation techniques, and the importance of caring for their mental and emotional well-being. These sessions encourage children to self-regulate and express their emotions in healthy, constructive ways.

Cooking Classes

Our cooking classes inspire creativity in the kitchen, teaching children to prepare simple meals while exploring diverse cultural dishes. These sessions enhance problem-solving skills as children learn to follow instructions and grasp early math concepts. By counting, measuring, and observing changes in ingredients, children are introduced to basic math and science, fostering critical thinking and curiosity.

Language Classes

Our language classes introduce children to the basics of different languages through fun, interactive activities. By exploring new cultures and traditions, these sessions foster curiosity, broaden worldviews, and encourage appreciation for diversity from an early age.
