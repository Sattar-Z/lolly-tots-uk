Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Lolly Tots Day Nursery and how we care for your little ones.

1. What age groups do you cater for?

We provide care for children aged 3 months to 5 years, offering tailored activities to support their development at every stage.

2. What are your opening hours?

Our nursery is open from 7:30 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. We offer flexible sessions to suit different family needs.

3. What is your settling-in process?

  • We offer a personalised settling-in process to help your child adjust comfortably, which can be requested or found in the welcome pack. This usually involves 1-3 visits to help your child adjust to their home away from home (periods may vary depending on each child).

  • You will be invited to spend time with your child at the nursery initially to ensure a smooth transition.

4. What is included in the nursery fees?

Our fees include freshly prepared meals and snacks, educational materials, and all extra-curricular activities, from outdoor play to cooking classes.

5. How do you communicate with parents?

We use daily communication books, emails, and termly parent-key worker meetings to keep you informed about your child's progress.

6. Can I visit the nursery before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage parents to schedule a visit to see our facilities and meet our staff before making a decision.
